Result & Precautions

Result

The PN junction diode exhibits distinct behavior under forward and reverse bias:
  • In forward bias, current increases exponentially with voltage.
  • In reverse bias, only a negligible leakage current flows.
  • This confirms the rectifying nature of the diode.

Precautions

  • Ensure correct polarity of the diode during connections.
  • Never exceed the maximum reverse voltage rating of the diode.
  • Use proper resistance values to limit current in forward bias.
  • Verify all connections before powering the circuit.
  • Handle all instruments and the diode carefully to avoid damage.
